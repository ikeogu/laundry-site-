About Us

Our brightly lit and conveniently located Darcies™ store is open for self-service customer laundry from early in the morning until late in the evening, and staffed by professionally trained - uniformed attendants whose goal is to make every customer's experience a pleasant one. Equipped with very large capacity machines that are consistently maintained and cleaned, laundry may be done in record time!! Each store also has semi-private areas with individual work areas and televisions.

Store attendants are at your service at all times and when not assisting a customer they are making sure that the store and all of the Darcies equipment is properly cleaned.

If you are tired of gathering up a bunch of quarters to feed laundry machines we have the solution. Laundry House™ allows customers the convenience of their own Laundry House™ Smart Card. The system that provides automatic savings, discounts and specials. Customers may simply pay cash at the time of service.

Laundry House™ Kids area has a place for the children to play or watch one of the many kids videos the store has available.

The store also offers carefully selected video games for your enjoyment as well as pay per use snack machines, massage chairs, internet terminals as well as free Laundry House™ Wi-Fi available for customer convenience.

Whether you have a very large business or just a small one, Laundry House commercial laundry service may be exactly what your laundry needs require. With Laundry House™ combination of customer service and flexibility many businesses have found Laundry House to be the perfect solution for their commercial needs.

Laundry House™ “Laundry Fresh Laundry Clean”, locally owned and operated since 2013 where no laundry project is ever too big or too small. We guarantee to do them all!
